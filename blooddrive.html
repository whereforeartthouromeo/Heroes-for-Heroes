Hospital Heroes 2018 Blood Drive

The Hospital Heroes team sponsored and promoted an American Red Cross blood drive at College View School in Glendale, California. The blood drive took place on December 21, 2018 from 1:00 to 6:00 P.M. and had 22 donations.
